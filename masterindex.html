So, why did we make this?

Color blindness is talked about disability, but has never quite been addressed in the most accessible ways. We as a team recognized this, and decided to come up with a user friendly guide and game to bring awareness to how people with color blindness see the world. Our name "Je vois" means I see in French, signifying the understanding between the general public and people with color blindness we strive for.

Addressing Color Blindness

We decided to use the most common color blindness, which was red-weak/protanomaly for our colorblind filter. This way, we would have a bigger reach, and easily guide new people into learning more about color blindness.
